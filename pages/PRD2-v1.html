JTBD-Driven Campaign Management & Attribution System

Product Requirements Document

Version 1.0 | April 14, 2025

1. Executive Summary

This Product Requirements Document (PRD) defines the specifications for developing an integrated Jobs-to-be-Done (JTBD) driven campaign management and attribution system. The system will enable marketing teams to create, manage, and optimize campaigns based on JTBD principles while providing comprehensive multi-touch attribution with machine learning capabilities.

The solution addresses key challenges faced by marketing and sales teams:

  • Disconnect between marketing content and customer job needs
  • Ineffective campaign targeting due to poor job alignment
  • Inability to accurately attribute conversions across multiple touchpoints
  • Lack of feedback loops between sales outcomes and marketing content

This system will integrate with existing website infrastructure, CRM systems (primarily Odoo), and content management processes to create a comprehensive JTBD-driven marketing ecosystem.

2. System Architecture

The system consists of eight primary components that work together to create a complete JTBD-driven campaign management and attribution ecosystem:

  1. JTBD Radar (Data Collection & Analysis)
  2. Force Analysis Engine
  3. Content Brain
  4. Guided Wizards
  5. Personalization Engine
  6. Component Library
  7. Conversion Layer
  8. Analytics & Optimization

Each component addresses specific aspects of the campaign management and attribution process while maintaining JTBD principles throughout.

3. Detailed Component Requirements

3.1 JTBD Radar (Data Collection & Analysis)

3.1.1 Data Sources

  • Requirement: System must collect data from multiple sources to identify customer jobs
  • Sources:
    • Reddit, industry forums, and social media platforms
    • CRM data (sales notes, call transcripts)
    • Manual inputs from sales and customer success teams
    • Website behavior and engagement metrics

3.1.2 Data Privacy Controls

  • Requirement: System must maintain compliance with data privacy regulations
  • Features:
    • Job statement anonymization layer
    • PII detection and removal
    • Standardized job statement templating
    • GDPR-compliant data storage
    • Data retention policies and management

3.1.3 Anxiety Detection Algorithm

  • Requirement: System must identify and classify customer anxieties related to specific jobs
  • Features:
    • NLP-based classification of pain points
    • Sentiment analysis for emotional triggers
    • Pattern recognition for recurring challenges
    • Urgency scoring based on language and frequency
    • Anxiety-to-job mapping

3.1.4 Processing Pipeline

  • Requirement: System must process raw data into structured job insights
  • Features:
    • Initial filtering with Python and RegEx
    • Classification using OpenAI API and custom models
    • Clustering for topic modeling
    • Prioritization with custom scoring algorithm
    • Regular retraining schedule for machine learning models

3.1.5 Rules-Based Fallback System

  • Requirement: System must maintain operation during AI service outages
  • Features:
    • Pattern-matching rules when AI is unavailable
    • Critical job detection during outages
    • Cached job statements for common patterns
    • Continuity during integration failures
    • Alert system for extended outages

3.2 Force Analysis Engine

3.2.1 Signal Processing

  • Requirement: System must identify and classify market signals relevant to customer jobs
  • Features:
    • Market signal categorization by source and reliability
    • Trigger event identification and classification
    • Momentum calculation algorithm
    • Risk level assessment and categorization
    • Signal strength weighting by source credibility

3.2.2 Decision Window Analysis

  • Requirement: System must determine optimal timing for campaign delivery
  • Features:
    • Trigger window determination (immediate to long-term)
    • Buying signal strength quantification
    • Contract loss and other significant event detection
    • Signal strength aggregation and scoring
    • Opportunity urgency classification

3.2.3 Force Item Management

  • Requirement: System must track and manage individual forces affecting customer decisions
  • Features:
    • Individual forces with strength scoring
    • Categorization by type (push, pull, inertia, anxiety)
    • Impact area classification
    • Visualization of force relationships
    • Force strength trending over time

3.2.4 Job Evolution Detection

  • Requirement: System must track how jobs change over time
  • Features:
    • Tracking job progression patterns
    • Identification of job maturity stages
    • Detection of job priority shifts
    • Content adaptation signals as jobs evolve
    • Historical job evolution visualization

4. Odoo CRM Integration Requirements

The system will integrate with Odoo CRM to store and manage JTBD-specific data. This section defines the custom fields, models, and integration points required.

4.1 Custom Fields

4.1.1 Job-Related Fields

  • jtbd_job_statement (Text): Primary job statement
  • jtbd_job_pattern_match (Many2many): Links to relevant job patterns
  • jtbd_job_quadrant (Selection): Classification of job type
  • jtbd_job_clarity_score (Integer): 0-100 rating of job clarity
  • jtbd_job_maturity_stage (Selection): Emerging, defining, evolving, mature
  • jtbd_job_priority_history (One2many): Historical record of job priority shifts

4.1.2 Outcome-Related Fields

  • jtbd_outcome_metric (Text): Primary outcome measurement
  • jtbd_outcome_mapping (Many2many): Links to outcome definitions
  • jtbd_white_space (Boolean): Indicates unexplored opportunities
  • jtbd_outcome_achievement (Float): Percentage of outcome achieved

4.1.3 Campaign-Related Fields

  • jtbd_automation_source (Selection): Source of lead (inbound, outbound)
  • jtbd_engagement_quality (Integer): 0-100 rating of engagement quality
  • jtbd_content_approval_state (Selection): Draft, review, approved
  • jtbd_content_alignment (Float): 0-100 rating of content-to-job alignment

4.1.4 Force-Related Fields

  • jtbd_intent_signals (One2many): Detected intent signals
  • jtbd_objection_forecast (One2many): Predicted objections
  • jtbd_force_strength (Float): Calculated force strength

4.1.5 Relationship Health Fields

  • jtbd_relationship_health_score (Integer): 0-100 overall relationship health
  • jtbd_knowledge_transfer_status (Selection): Not started, in progress, completed

4.1.6 Implementation Fields

  • jtbd_implementation_readiness (Integer): 0-100 client readiness score
  • jtbd_resource_allocation_status (Selection): Insufficient, minimal, adequate, optimal

4.2 Custom Models

4.2.1 jtbd.job.pattern

  • name (Char): Pattern identifier
  • description (Text): Pattern description
  • vertical (Many2one): Industry vertical
  • frequency (Integer): Occurrence frequency
  • example_statements (One2many): Example job statements
  • related_patterns (Many2many): Related job patterns

4.2.2 jtbd.outcome.definition

  • name (Char): Outcome identifier
  • description (Text): Outcome description
  • metric_type (Selection): Quantitative, qualitative
  • measurement_method (Text): How outcome is measured
  • benchmark_value (Float): Typical value for comparison

4.2.3 jtbd.content.alignment

  • content_id (Many2one): Related content
  • job_pattern_id (Many2one): Related job pattern
  • alignment_score (Float): 0-100 alignment rating
  • improvement_suggestions (Text): How to improve alignment
  • performance_metrics (One2many): Performance data

4.2.4 jtbd.job.portfolio

  • lead_id (Many2one): Related lead/opportunity
  • job_patterns (Many2many): Associated job patterns
  • priority_weights (One2many): Priority weight for each job
  • interrelationships (One2many): How jobs relate to each other
  • conflicts (One2many): Conflicting job needs

4.2.5 jtbd.knowledge.transfer

  • name (Char): Transfer identifier
  • source_team (Many2one): Team transferring knowledge
  • target_team (Many2one): Team receiving knowledge
  • job_patterns (Many2many): Relevant job patterns
  • components (One2many): Transfer components
  • completion_status (Float): Percentage complete
  • verification_steps (One2many): Verification checkpoints

4.2.6 jtbd.job.evolution

  • job_pattern_id (Many2one): Related job pattern
  • previous_states (One2many): Historical states
  • transition_triggers (One2many): What caused transitions
  • maturity_indicators (One2many): Indicators of maturity level
  • evolution_timeline (One2many): Timeline of evolution

19. Appendices

Appendix A: Glossary of Terms

  • JTBD (Jobs-to-be-Done): A framework for understanding customer needs based on the “jobs” they need to accomplish
  • ODI (Outcome-Driven Innovation): A methodology for creating products and services based on customer-desired outcomes
  • Job Statement: A structured description of a customer need in the format “I want to [action] so I can [outcome]”
  • Force Analysis: Evaluation of the pushes, pulls, anxieties, and habits that influence customer decisions
  • Job Quadrant: Classification of jobs into categories (functional, emotional, social, etc.)
  • Multi-touch Attribution: Tracking and assigning value to multiple customer touchpoints in the conversion process
  • Content Alignment: The degree to which content addresses specific customer jobs
  • Job Clarity Score: Measure of how well a job statement is understood and defined
  • Edge Functions: Server functions that run at the edge of the network for low-latency personalization
  • Job Pattern: Recurring structure in job statements that can be identified and categorized
