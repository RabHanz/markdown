Stone & Soil

A design system rooted in the raw beauty of mountains, earth, and the elemental forces that shape our world.

Philosophy

Built on Bedrock

This system draws from the permanence of stone, the richness of earth, and the warmth of weathered materials. Every element carries weight and presence—nothing floats, nothing wavers. Colors emerge from clay and mineral, typography stands firm like carved inscription, and motion settles with the gravity of falling stone.
